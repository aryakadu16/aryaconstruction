The Brewyard

A yard for slow coffee, in the middle of LCR.

Tucked into a bungalow just off Law College Road, The Brewyard is built around one idea — coffee deserves the same patience as any good meal. We source, roast and brew in-house, and hand every cup to you the way our tasters approved it.

Pull up a chair in the courtyard, watch a pour-over happen at the bar, or just come for the quiet and the wifi. Either way, the beans were picked with you in mind.

How We Brew

Every pour, timed and ratioed.

A V60 at the bar, start to finish — the four steps behind every filter cup we serve.

1:16 0:00 — 0:30

Bloom

Grounds meet just enough water to release CO₂ and open up the aroma.

1st 0:30 — 1:15

First Pour

A slow, centre-out spiral brings the bed up to half its total water.

2nd 1:15 — 2:00

Second Pour

Remaining water goes in, keeping the bed level steady throughout.

3:00 Drawdown

Serve

Full drawdown by three minutes, poured straight into your cup.
