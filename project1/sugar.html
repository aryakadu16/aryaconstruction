Our Story

Baked in a home kitchen, boxed with heart.

The Sugar Loop started as weekend baking for friends in Lonavala and turned into something people started asking for by name. Every order is still made in small batches, so what reaches your door is what came out of the oven that day — no shortcuts, no mixes.

From fudgy brownies to birthday cakes with your name piped on, every box is packed like it's for someone we know. Because usually, it is.
